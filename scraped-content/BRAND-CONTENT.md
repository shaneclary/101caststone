# PROCESS: THE LINTEL METHOD

## Dialogue
We listen first. Understanding the architecture, the setting, the vision that drives the project. This conversation shapes everything that follows.

## Mould
Custom moulds engineered for precision. Whether matching existing profiles or creating new vocabularies, the mould captures intent in negative space.

## Cast
Mineral aggregates, Portland cement, careful formulation. The pour fills the mould; the cure develops strength. Materials sourced for permanence and patina.

## Finish
Hand-tooled edges. Applied patinas where appropriate. The surface refined until it whispers rather than shouts. Quality control before every piece leaves the workshop.
